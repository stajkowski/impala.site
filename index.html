WHAT CAN I LEARN?

Master Shot Psychology

When aiming at the center target, some archers experience psycological conditions. Overcome these conditions and improve shot execution by mastering shot psychology.

Proper Form

Develop consistent form when holding any bow. Control vertical position and promote muscle memory.

Aim Stability

Laser light assists in stability control. Learn how to aim and sight properly in a consistent manner while discovering the cause of instability in your shot.

Draw Length Control

Simulated arrow provides consistency in reacting to a clicker for recurve archers. Practice finger release under identical simulated tension.

Compound Anchor Position

Develop a consistent anchor and commit to muscle memory. Simulated tension provides a consistent backwall for reliable (Compound) training.

Back Tension

Develop back tension for consistent feel and relase. Learn how to keep back tension under control and promote muscle memory.

ABOUT US

OUR STORY

After more than 35 years of experience in archery, both as competitors and as coaches, we drew on all that we had learned to create the AK&Kirilov eXtreme archery bow simulator. We've focused on every detail of the archery learning and training process to craft a training device that will help you improve your archery expertise and achieve lasting excellence.

Krasi Kirilov

FOUNDER/OWNER

Krasi Kirilov is a recurve & compound shooter who has coached archery for over 30 years. He has competed in many national and international competitions around the world throughout his career, including the National Bulgarian Archery.
